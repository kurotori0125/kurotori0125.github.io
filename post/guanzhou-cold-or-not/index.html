【GuanZhou】Cold or Not

2018-06-09 / 2 min read

没搞完的英翻

"Fuck, Guan, how the hell people could stand this cold in the south! I bet this is much more horrible than your poker-face！ " ZX trembles with cold，putting out his hand to blow his frosty nose and immediately retracting it in a clenching fist to his cuff.

GHF gives ZX a glimpse and turns his head, poker-faced.

According to Behavioristic psychology, it usually takes 21 to 90 days to turn some habits or concepts into "naturally and causally" ZX has followed his so-call teacher ( already not now but lover ) GHF for a dozen years, it should have been his habitual concept rooted in his subconsciousness that he always says yes only to GHF. Even in those special days, when face to face, ZX never said no directly. But change happened. Following the end of the case, GHF and ZX now spend almost every second together (just turn 17、18 hours into 24 hours, people say ), who could foresee that would change ZX's mind? As the youth in the police station said in the past: "Captain Zhou is judging everyone but GHF" But now it seems already not the truth.

Just like a pot of hot sticky porridge was cooked on the stove, bubbling and exploding softly, if you stir it with the spoon, it can keep quiet for a while.

GHF is not so willing to admit that he enjoys ZX's change and his new temper.
